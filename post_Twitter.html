Recruiters are on Twitter—are you?

  • Career
April 21, 2019


When we think of social media sites for job searching, we often think of LinkedIn. That’s not surprising—in a 2015 survey by the Society for Human Resource Management, more than half of hiring professionals surveyed reported LinkedIn was their most successful site for recruitment. But LinkedIn isn’t the only option. For job applicants, casting a wide net is a great way to find more opportunities and engage directly with recruiters. In this presentation, we will briefly explore Twitter’s place in your job seeking strategy.
